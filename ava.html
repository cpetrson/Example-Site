Virtual Academy Website

Website for a small but growing Nonprofit

Summer 2024 - Spring 2025

Extending the capabilities of a goDaddy Website+Marketing site through HTML and CSS custom code, I maintained the website, grew the customer list, sent weekly newsletters, and added custom HTML to provide 'front-door' sign-in level account-accessed pages.

Project:

This website was a good project from the standpoint that I experienced the limitations of the 'out-of-the-box' goDaddy website builder and account management. I wrote HTML and CSS code customization to give members access to community pages with their own internal navigation structure. One feature I liked was the email campaign feedback on when emails were opened and whether links were clicked in the newsletters.
