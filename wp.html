Building a Custom WordPress Theme

(And Putting up a Live Website)

2020 - Present (Rebuilt Summer 2023)

After some research into hosting options, various plans, and developer-friendly hosting, I chose hosting.com's Drive plan. After installing WordPress and getting accustomed to cPanel and the wp-config panel, I was off and running. First I built a WordPress child-theme for a family reunion website. This was a sandbox to learn WordPress, cPanel, the themes folder, "The Loop," hooks, and classic PHP and HTML interleaving.

Site Login Page

I was still making modifications to the child theme when I picked up Brad Schiff's tutorial (see below) and saw how much better his approach was to WordPress Theme Development. I scrapped my first attempt (the child theme) and rebuilt the site in 2023 using his 'Fictional University' model site as a starting point.
